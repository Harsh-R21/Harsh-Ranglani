Financial Analysis of JPMorgan Chase & Stock Price Correlation

Project Report by Harsh Ranglani

Overview

This project analyzes the relationship between the quarterly net income of JPMorgan Chase and its average stock prices from 2022 to 2024. The goal is to examine the correlation between net income and stock prices and provide visualizations to illustrate this relationship.

Data Collection

We collected quarterly financial statements focusing on net income, revenue, assets, and liabilities. Additionally, historical stock prices were obtained from Yahoo Finance and resampled to quarterly data.

Data Analysis

The correlation analysis between net income and average stock prices was conducted, and key findings were derived based on this analysis. Here are the main steps taken:

  1. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing
  2. Merging Financial Data with Stock Prices
  3. Correlation Calculation
  4. Visualization of Results

Visualizations

The following visualizations were created to illustrate the relationship between financial metrics and stock performance:

Key Findings

Our analysis revealed a strong positive correlation (0.85) between net income and stock prices. Both metrics showed a rising trend from Q1 2022 to Q3 2024, indicating that financial performance significantly influences stock market valuation.
